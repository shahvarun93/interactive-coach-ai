Observability for System Design Interviews

Three Pillars

  • Logs: structured, queryable, correlated by request ID.
  • Metrics: request rate, latency, error rate, saturation.
  • Traces: end-to-end view across services.

Key Metrics

  • SLIs/SLOs: availability, p95/p99 latency, error rates.
  • Per-service dashboards and alerts.

Design Checklist

  • Add request IDs / correlation IDs at the edge.
  • Define critical paths and instrument them first.
  • Plan for alerting on symptoms, not just causes.

Interview Checklist

  • Mention where you’d add metrics, logs, and traces.
  • Explain how you’d debug a high latency or error spike.
  • Call out how observability helps with capacity planning and incident response.
