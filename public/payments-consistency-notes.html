Payments Consistency Notes

Key Requirements

  • No double-charging, no lost charges.
  • Strong auditability and traceability.
  • Eventual consistency acceptable in some views, not in ledger.

Patterns

  • Idempotency keys for payment requests.
  • Transactional outbox for integrating with external systems.
  • Separate ledger (immutable) from read models.

Failure Scenarios

  • Payment provider times out but charge went through.
  • Retries causing duplicate callbacks.
  • Reconciliation with provider reports.

Interview Checklist

  • Clarify money flows and who is system of record.
  • Discuss idempotency and how to avoid duplicate charges.
  • Mention reconciliation and audits.
  • Be explicit about where strong vs eventual consistency is acceptable.
