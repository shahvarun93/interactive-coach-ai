Designing a Global Feed

Feed Models

  • Fan-out on write: push to follower feeds at write time.
  • Fan-out on read: compute feed on demand at read time.
  • Hybrid: precompute for heavy users, compute on read for light users.

Key Concerns

  • Latency vs freshness vs cost of recomputation.
  • Personalization vs simple chronological ordering.
  • Hot users / celebrities and write amplification.

Data Model Sketch

  • users, follows, posts, feeds (per-user fan-out).
  • Indexes on follows(follower_id), posts(author_id, created_at).

Interview Checklist

  • Clarify: global vs per-user vs topic feeds.
  • Explain which fan-out strategy you choose and why.
  • Discuss pagination, ranking, and caching.
  • Consider backfill, deletions, and abuse / spam.
